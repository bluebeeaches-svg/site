Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How does Blue Beaches determine if a beach is safe (Green)?

A: Our app aggregates official public safety reports, lifeguard notifications, and local environmental data to display real-time statuses for monitored beach zones.

Q: Is the weather information accurate?

A: Yes, weather data and localized temperatures are synced live via premium, high-accuracy meteorological API providers specialized in coastal regions.

Q: Do I need to create an account to use the application?

A: No, Blue Beaches values your speed and convenience. All core features, maps, and status updates are fully accessible immediately upon installing without any mandatory signup.

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which Blue Beaches collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users of the Blue Beaches mobile application.

Blue Beaches is built with privacy-by-design. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or contacts list. You can utilize the application completely anonymously.

2. Non-personal Device Data & Location

To provide accurate, close-range beach recommendations and local weather updates, our application may check your device's approximate geolocation. Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided personal data, we immediately delete this from our records.
